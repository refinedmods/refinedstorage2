= Refined Storage 2 released
:type: article
:description: A major milestone in the evolution of Refined Storage
:date: 2025-09-29

Five years after the very first commit for Refined Storage 2, it has left beta and is officially released.
This marks a complete rewrite of the mod, laying a stronger foundation for the future.

== Beta period

The beta lasted 6 months and was an intensive phase of testing and refinement.

Across this period, 77 bugs were resolved. The final betas saw only minor, hard-to-reproduce bugs being fixed, and today the issue tracker stands at **zero** open bugs.

This stability gave us the confidence to move forward with a full release.

Special thanks to all the testers and people reporting issues on GitHub and Discord.

== Goal

Refined Storage 2 has been rebuilt from the ground up with the following goals in mind:

- A strong focus on code quality
- Long-term maintainability
- Mod loader independence (support for Fabric and NeoForge)
- A complete rethinking and modernization of nearly every feature
